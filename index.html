About

Code Odyssey 2.0 is a 24-hour hackathon organized by the Computer Society of India, Students' Chapter KJSIT, with endless creativity. Students are challenged to demonstrate their knowledge by creating projects that follow their passions. Spending time on projects outside the classroom is the best way to not only learn what you love but also find new interests. You have 24 hours to come up with something new and show us what you've got in a universe with endless possibilities!

Abstract Note

  • 1. Interested teams will have to register first at Devfolio and then through a Google Form in any of the following categories applicable to the project they will build. Participants are free to choose their own problem statement.
  • 2. During registration, participants have to submit the relevant details of the project they will build in the form of a PowerPoint Presentation as an abstract. The abstract should contain the following:
    • a. Team Details
    • b. Problem Statement Title
    • c Problem Statement - Motivation, Description, Need, Feasibility, Approach, and Unique Selling Point.
    • d. Technical Stack
    • e. Any other relevant information related to the project
  • 3. Participants can choose their own PowerPoint Presentation Template Design. The maximum number of slides allowed is 8.
  • 4. Abstract should be submitted via Google Form only, failing which your submission will remain incomplete.
  • 5. There will be an elimination round based on the abstracts received where “TOP 25” teams will qualify for the final round.
  • 6. Final Date to submit the abstract is 11:59 PM, 31st March 2023, and will not be extended further.
  • 7. Results of the Abstract Round will be released on 1st April 2023.

    FAQ ?

    What is a hackathon?
    Hacking is a creative way to solve a problem, and hackathons are innovation marathons where students from around the country who have an interest in technology come together to learn, build & share their creations over the course of a weekend in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere.
    How many people per team?
    Teams can range anywhere from 1-4 people. If you do not have a team before the event, worry not - you can find one on our handy Discord server.
    What is the registration fee for the event?
    Code Odyssey is free for all hackers! We provide our hackers with tools, swags, and prizes without any cost through our generous sponsors.
    When can we start working on the solution?
    You can start working on the solution strictly with the commencment of the hackathon with all the other participants.
    Will there be food?
    Yes, delicious food and beverages will be provided throughout the event.
    How do I reach KJSIT(venue)?
    KJSIT is well connected a kilometer from Sion Station and just 200 metres from Chunabhatti Station . Also you can reach the venue by the eastern express highway by road as well.
